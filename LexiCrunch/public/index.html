LexiCrunch Instructions

You are given 20 letter tiles at the start of the game. You must make a word from the 10 tiles in the bottom row. The top row is not available for use but is visible. Once a valid word has been submitted, the top row will move down and can be used to make the next word. A new top row will come down from off-screen. You will have 1 minute to make up to 10 words, 1 word per row of letter tiles. You will be scored based on the length and novelty of the word you have submitted. Once you have made 10 words, or when the timer expires, whichever comes first, the game is over. Click the button below to clear these instructions and play the game.
